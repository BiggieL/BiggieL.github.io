  • Game 3 - 3D Precision Platformer

    • You are a poor sap who sold their soul to the corporate overlords in order to secure a better afterlife. Look where that got ya. You wake up in an arrary of office cubicles once more, and are greeted with a harsh reality. Your afterlife will be the same as your previous one: eat, sleep, work, repeat. That is, until you exit the endless cubicles into an indescribable realm. Take hold of a grappling hook and begin your platforming journey to escape the monotony, accompanied by a zany three headed dog. Use the core mechanic, dimensional shift, to solve puzzles and complete difficult platforming sequences. Each level has its own unique twist and flair, and I am quite proud with how it turned out. I was in charge of the main movement, camera, and physics, which were a nightmare to iron out. I was also in charge of bugfixing, gamefeel mechanics, sound effects, and my very own level. In my level, you must navigate through an abandoned fan shaft, filled with glitchy scenery and complex platforming. Use your dimensional shift to swap to a world where the fan is still working, and boost yourself up through this labrynth. In order to get out of the fan shaft, you need to deactivate the top fan in the alternate dimension, which means you have to do some diffiuclt platforming back down in the shaft in order to deactivate the breaker boxes. This helped push my creativty in level design and helped me create something both difficult and fun.
